Bomberrage Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Big Jan Games ("we," "our," or "us") develops Bomberrage, a multiplayer action game. This Privacy Policy explains how we may collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play Bomberrage.

About Bomberrage: Bomberrage is a multiplayer action game that may use third-party services for certain functions. Depending on the features you use, data may be shared with service providers.

2. Information We May Collect

2.1 Game Data

During gameplay, we may collect:

  • Match statistics and game progress
  • Gameplay preferences and settings
  • Session information
  • Platform and device information
  • Performance and technical data

2.2 Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device specifications and performance data
  • Operating system information
  • Error reports and crash logs
  • Network connectivity data

2.3 Third-Party Service Data

If you use multiplayer features, we may receive account and authentication data from third-party services as needed for game functionality.

3. How We May Use Data

We may use information for Bomberrage operations including:

  • Game functionality and performance
  • Technical support and troubleshooting
  • Game improvement and balancing
  • Security and anti-cheat measures
  • Communication with players when necessary

4. Data Sharing

We may share information in limited circumstances:

  • With third-party services for multiplayer functionality
  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect game security and integrity
  • With service providers under data protection agreements

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for multiplayer functionality. Data may be shared with these services as needed for game features.

Third-party services have their own privacy policies and data practices. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information about how they handle your data.

6. Our Security Measures

We may implement security measures to protect data including:

  • Access controls for our systems
  • Security monitoring where applicable
  • Regular security reviews of our systems
  • Data protection measures as appropriate

If a security incident affects our data collection, we will provide appropriate notification as required by law.

7. Data Retention

We may retain data for periods including:

  • Game data while you actively use Bomberrage
  • Technical data for reasonable periods for game improvement
  • Support communications as needed for customer service
  • Other data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes

Third-party services have their own data retention practices as outlined in their respective privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding data we collect including:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your data
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Objection to processing

For Bomberrage-specific data requests, contact us at contact@bigjangames.website. For third-party service data practices, contact those services directly.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate parental consent as required by law.

If third-party services are used, please refer to their privacy policies for their age requirements and practices. For concerns about children's use of Bomberrage, contact us at contact@bigjangames.website.

10. International Data Processing

Data may be processed internationally depending on the services used and your location.

Third-party services may have their own international data processing practices as outlined in their privacy policies.

11. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We may notify you of significant changes through the game or our website.

Continued use of Bomberrage after policy updates may indicate acceptance of the revised terms.
