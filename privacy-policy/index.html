Bomberrage Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Big Jan Games ("we," "our," or "us") develops and operates Bomberrage, a multiplayer action game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play Bomberrage, which uses Epic Online Services (EOS) for multiplayer functionality, player authentication, and cross-platform features.

About Bomberrage: Bomberrage is a fast-paced multiplayer action game featuring strategic bombing gameplay, real-time player matchmaking, competitive lobbies, and cross-platform multiplayer support powered by Epic Games Services.

2. Information We Collect in Bomberrage

2.1 Epic Account Information

When you sign in to Bomberrage using Epic Account Services, Epic Games collects and shares with us:

  • Epic Account ID and display name
  • Account authentication tokens
  • Friends list information (if you choose to connect with friends)
  • Account country/region for regional matchmaking
  • Account creation date and verification status

2.2 Bomberrage Gameplay Data

During Bomberrage gameplay sessions, we collect:

  • Match statistics (wins, losses, eliminations, placement)
  • Player progression data (levels, unlocks, achievements)
  • Lobby participation and matchmaking preferences
  • In-game communication and voice chat usage
  • Session duration and frequency of play
  • Platform information (PC, mobile device specifications)
  • Network latency and connection quality data

2.3 Technical and Device Information

To provide optimal Bomberrage performance, we automatically collect:

  • Device hardware specifications and performance metrics
  • Operating system version and graphics capabilities
  • Game crash reports and error logs
  • Network connectivity information for matchmaking optimization
  • Input device preferences (controller, keyboard/mouse)

3. How We Use Your Bomberrage Data

We use your information specifically for Bomberrage operations:

  • Multiplayer Matchmaking: Connect you with other players based on skill level and geographic location
  • Cross-Platform Play: Enable gameplay between different platforms using Epic Online Services
  • Player Authentication: Verify your identity and maintain secure access to your Bomberrage account
  • Friends and Social Features: Connect with Epic friends list for party formation and invitations
  • Game Balance: Analyze gameplay data to improve weapon balance and map design
  • Anti-Cheat Protection: Monitor for unfair play and maintain competitive integrity
  • Performance Optimization: Improve game stability and reduce latency based on technical data
  • Player Support: Troubleshoot technical issues and assist with account problems
  • Game Updates: Deliver content updates and new features to enhance Bomberrage experience

4. Epic Games Services Data Sharing

IMPORTANT: Bomberrage uses Epic Online Services, which means your data is shared with Epic Games to enable multiplayer functionality:

  • Epic Games Integration: Your Epic Account data, matchmaking preferences, and gameplay statistics are processed by Epic Games' servers
  • Cross-Platform Features: Epic Games facilitates friend connections, party systems, and voice chat across different platforms
  • Authentication Services: Epic Games verifies your account and manages login sessions for Bomberrage
  • Matchmaking Data: Epic Games processes your skill ratings, geographic location, and connection quality for optimal match creation

We also share information in these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent for specific features
  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • To protect the security and integrity of Bomberrage gameplay
  • With technical service providers under strict data protection agreements

5. Epic Online Services Integration

Bomberrage is built on Epic Online Services (EOS) infrastructure. This integration provides:

Epic Account Services

  • Secure player authentication and account management
  • Cross-platform friend connections and social features
  • Unified player identity across Epic Games ecosystem
  • Account security features including two-factor authentication

Multiplayer Infrastructure

  • Real-time matchmaking based on skill and connection quality
  • Dedicated game servers for low-latency Bomberrage matches
  • Lobby system for party formation and private matches
  • Voice chat integration for team communication

Epic Games Privacy Policy: Epic Games has their own privacy policy governing Epic Account Services and EOS data processing. We encourage you to review Epic's privacy policy at https://www.epicgames.com/site/en-US/privacypolicy.

6. Bomberrage Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect Bomberrage player data:

  • Epic Games Security: Player authentication and account security managed by Epic Games' enterprise-grade systems
  • Encrypted Data: All gameplay data transmitted between Bomberrage and Epic Online Services is encrypted
  • Anti-Cheat Protection: Advanced monitoring systems prevent cheating and protect fair play
  • Secure Servers: Bomberrage matches run on Epic Games' secure, dedicated gaming infrastructure
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access controls limit who can view player data
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing for Bomberrage systems

Incident Response: If a security incident affects Bomberrage player data, we will notify affected players and relevant authorities as required by law.

7. Bomberrage Data Retention

We retain your Bomberrage data for the following periods:

  • Active Players: Game progress, statistics, and achievements are retained while your Epic Account remains active
  • Inactive Accounts: Gameplay data may be archived after 2 years of inactivity
  • Match History: Recent match data is retained for 1 year for anti-cheat and game improvement purposes
  • Technical Logs: Crash reports and performance data are retained for 6 months
  • Communication Records: Support communications are retained for 3 years

Epic Games Retention: Epic Account data retention is governed by Epic Games' privacy policy. Deletion of your Epic Account will remove your access to Bomberrage and associated game data.

8. Your Rights and Managing Epic Account Data

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your Bomberrage data:

  • Access your Bomberrage gameplay data and statistics
  • Correct inaccurate player information
  • Request deletion of your Bomberrage account data
  • Restrict processing of certain gameplay information
  • Export your game progress data
  • Object to certain data processing activities

Managing Your Epic Account

For Epic Account-related data and privacy settings:

  • Account Management: Access your Epic Account settings through the Epic Games launcher or website
  • Privacy Controls: Manage friend visibility, online status, and social features in Epic Account settings
  • Data Deletion: To delete your Epic Account (which will affect Bomberrage access), contact Epic Games support
  • Communication Preferences: Control Epic Games marketing communications through your account preferences
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Enable additional security features in your Epic Account security settings

Contact Information: For Bomberrage-specific data requests, contact us at contact@bigjangames.website. For Epic Account issues, contact Epic Games support directly.

9. Children's Privacy and Age Requirements

Bomberrage requires an Epic Account for multiplayer functionality. Epic Games' age requirements apply:

  • Age 13+: Players aged 13-17 need parental permission for Epic Account creation
  • Parental Controls: Epic Games provides parental controls for managing children's accounts and communication features
  • Data Collection: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without proper parental consent
  • Communication Features: Parents can restrict voice chat and text communication in Bomberrage through Epic Account settings

If you are a parent and believe your child has created an Epic Account or is playing Bomberrage without permission, please contact Epic Games support and us immediately at contact@bigjangames.website.

10. International Data Processing

Bomberrage operates globally using Epic Online Services infrastructure:

  • Global Servers: Epic Games operates Bomberrage servers worldwide to provide low-latency gameplay
  • Data Centers: Your gameplay data may be processed in Epic Games data centers in the United States, Europe, and other regions
  • Cross-Border Transfers: Player data is transferred internationally to enable cross-platform and global matchmaking features
  • Legal Safeguards: Epic Games maintains appropriate legal agreements and safeguards for international data transfers

For specific information about Epic Games' international data processing practices, please refer to Epic's privacy policy.

11. Updates to This Bomberrage Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in Bomberrage features or Epic Online Services integration:

  • Game Updates: New Bomberrage features may require additional data collection, which will be explained in policy updates
  • Epic Services Changes: Updates to Epic Online Services integration may affect data processing practices
  • Notification Method: Significant changes will be announced in-game and on our website
  • Continued Play: Continuing to play Bomberrage after policy updates indicates acceptance of the revised terms

We recommend reviewing this policy periodically, especially before major Bomberrage updates.
