About Bomberrage

💣

Explosive Multiplayer Battles!

Step into the chaos of Bomberrage, a fast-paced, action-packed multiplayer game where strategy and reflexes decide who survives! Battle against smart AI or challenge friends in explosive skirmishes, where every second counts!

🔥 Intense Multiplayer Battles – Face off against AI or real players in dynamic bomb fights!
🧠 Challenging AI Opponents – Adapt and outsmart relentless enemies!
🗺 Ever-Changing Arenas – Procedurally generated maps keep battles fresh!

System Requirements

Minimum Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2500K / AMD FX-6300
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 1050 (2GB) / AMD Radeon RX 560
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection
  • Storage: 4 GB available space

Recommended Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-8700K / AMD Ryzen 7 2700X
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA RTX 2060 / AMD RX 5700 XT
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection
  • Storage: 4 GB available space (SSD recommended)
